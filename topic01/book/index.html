QlikView installation

QlikView is a data discovery tool which we will use to transform and load data sources which we will then analyse and create dashboards for viewing.

QlikView is available to each of you on an academic license which lasts for 1 academic year. There is only one installation per student please.

Go to http://www.qlik.com

You need to create a QlikView account, also known as your single sign on.

Choose Login and then Not Registered? Get a QlikView Account

Complete the form, please ensure the Company field has Waterford Institute of Technology, Job Title should have Student entered, and in the email please use your wit student gmail account. If you don't have access to your student gmail account you can use you work or personal email address.

Login to Qlik with the details emailed to you. Next dowload the QlikView software.

Choose your operating system, accept the licence agreement and hit download.

Once it is downloaded run the executable to install QlikView.
